✨ Dwarka

Dwarka is one of the seven ancient sacred cities of India and is located on the western coast of Gujarat. It is famously known as the kingdom of Lord Krishna.

🌟 History & Significance

Dwarka is believed to have been the residence of Lord Krishna, making it one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites. The Dwarkadhish Temple, dedicated to Krishna, is renowned for its intricate architecture and spiritual importance.

🛕 What to Expect

Visitors can enjoy the spiritual atmosphere of the temples, attend daily aartis, explore local markets, and visit nearby attractions like Bet Dwarka Island and Rukmini Devi Temple.

📍 Location & Travel Tips

Dwarka is accessible by road, rail, and air via nearby airports. The best time to visit is between October and March, avoiding the hot summer months.
