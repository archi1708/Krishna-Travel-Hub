✨ Somnath

Somnath is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on the Arabian Sea coast of Gujarat.

🌟 History & Significance

The Somnath Temple has been destroyed and rebuilt several times in history, symbolizing resilience and faith. It holds immense religious importance and attracts pilgrims from all over India.

🛕 What to Expect

Visitors can witness the stunning temple architecture, attend evening aartis, enjoy the nearby beach, and explore cultural events that showcase the region's heritage.

📍 Location & Travel Tips

Somnath is accessible by road, rail, and air (via nearby airports). The best time to visit is from October to March for comfortable weather.
