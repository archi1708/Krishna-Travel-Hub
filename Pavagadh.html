✨ Pavagadh

Pavagadh is a famous hill station in Gujarat, known for its historic fort and the Kalika Mata Temple perched atop the hill.

🌟 History & Significance

The Pavagadh Fort is part of the UNESCO World Heritage Champaner-Pavagadh Archaeological Park. The Kalika Mata Temple is a major pilgrimage spot attracting devotees year-round.

🛕 What to Expect

Visitors can enjoy a cable car ride to the temple, explore the fort ruins, and take in stunning views of the lush surroundings. The area also hosts vibrant fairs and festivals.

📍 Location & Travel Tips

Pavagadh is accessible by road and rail, located near Vadodara. The ideal time to visit is during the cooler months from October to March.
